Veggie-Packed Whole Wheat Pasta Primavera

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (about 225g) whole wheat pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced/li>
  • 1 medium zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cups spinach leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, thinly sliced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook Whole Wheat Pasta:
    (Boil the whole wheat pasta in a large pot of salted water according to package instructions. Cook until al dente, then drain and set aside.)
  2. Prepare Vegetables:
    (While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.)
  3. Sauté Veggies:
    (Add zucchini, carrot, and bell pepper to the skillet. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still vibrant.)
  4. Add Cherry Tomatoes and Spinach:
    (Stir in cherry tomatoes and chopped spinach. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes are softened, and the spinach is wilted.)
  5. Combine with Pasta:
    (Add the cooked whole wheat pasta to the skillet, tossing everything together until well combined. If needed, drizzle a bit more olive oil to prevent sticking.)
  6. Season and Garnish:
    (Season the pasta primavera with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir in fresh basil for a burst of flavor and vibrant color. Adjust seasoning as needed.)
  7. Serve Warm:
    (Plate the Veggie-Packed Whole Wheat Pasta Primavera and, if desired, sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese for an extra layer of savory goodness.)
  8. Enjoy Your Healthy Pasta:
    (Serve this nutritious and colorful pasta dish immediately. Enjoy the wholesome combination of whole wheat pasta and a variety of fresh vegetables, making it a satisfying and healthy meal.)

This Veggie-Packed Whole Wheat Pasta Primavera is not only delicious but also a guilt-free way to enjoy your favorite pasta. Packed with nutrients and vibrant colors, it's a celebration of wholesome ingredients that will leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

Honey Glazed Nut and Fruit Medley

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews)
  • 1 cup dried fruits (apricots, figs, cranberries)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup seeds (pumpkin seeds or sunflower seeds, optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    (Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C).)
  2. Prepare the Nuts:
    (If your nuts are raw, spread them out on a baking sheet. Roast them in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes or until they become fragrant and lightly golden. Keep an eye on them to prevent burning.)
  3. Prepare the Dried Fruits:
    (Chop the dried fruits into bite-sized pieces. If you're using large dried fruits like figs, quarter them for even distribution in the mix.)
  4. Combine Nuts and Dried Fruits:
    (In a large mixing bowl, combine the roasted nuts and chopped dried fruits. If you're adding seeds, mix them in as well.)
  5. Prepare the Honey Glaze:
    (In a small saucepan over low heat, melt the unsalted butter. Add the honey, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Stir until the mixture is well combined and slightly thickened.)
  6. Coat the Nut and Fruit Mix:
    (Pour the honey glaze over the nut and fruit mix. Use a spatula to ensure all the ingredients are evenly coated with the sweet and spiced glaze.)
  7. Bake in the Oven:
    (Spread the coated nut and fruit mix back onto the baking sheet, and return it to the oven for an additional 5-7 minutes. This helps the glaze to caramelize and create a delightful crunch.)
  8. Cool and Break Apart:
    (Allow the mixture to cool on the baking sheet. As it cools, the honey glaze will harden, creating a satisfying crunch. Once cooled, break apart any clusters and mix well.)
  9. Serve and Enjoy:
    (Transfer your Honey Glazed Nut and Fruit Medley to a serving bowl or store it in an airtight container for later. Enjoy it as a snack on its own, or sprinkle it over yogurt, ice cream, or salads for added texture and sweetness.)

This Honey Glazed Nut and Fruit Medley is a delightful combination of crunchy nuts, chewy dried fruits, and a sweet-spiced glaze, making it a perfect snack for any occasion.

Mango Avocado Summer Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens (spinach, arugula, or your choice)
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 ripe avocado, peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Greens:
    (Wash and dry the mixed salad greens, then arrange them on a serving plate or bowl.)
  2. Dice Mango and Slice Avocado:
    (Peel and dice the ripe mango, and slice the ripe avocado. Arrange them on top of the salad greens.)
  3. Add Red Onion, Cherry Tomatoes, and Cucumber:
    (Thinly slice the red onion, halve the cherry tomatoes, and slice the cucumber. Scatter them over the salad.)
  4. Sprinkle Feta Cheese:
    (Crumble the feta cheese over the salad. Feta adds a creamy and tangy element to balance the sweetness of the mango.)
  5. Prepare the Dressing:
    (In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the quantities based on your preference.)
  6. Drizzle the Dressing:
    (Drizzle the dressing over the salad, ensuring it coats the ingredients evenly. Toss gently to combine and coat the salad with the delicious dressing.)
  7. Garnish with Fresh Basil (Optional):
    (For an extra burst of freshness, garnish the salad with fresh basil leaves. This step is optional but highly recommended for a delightful aroma.)
  8. Serve Immediately:
    (Serve the Mango Avocado Summer Salad immediately, savoring the vibrant colors and flavors. It makes a refreshing side dish or a light and satisfying meal on its own.)

This easy-to-make Mango Avocado Summer Salad is a celebration of seasonal produce, bringing together the sweetness of mango, creaminess of avocado, and a medley of vibrant vegetables. Enjoy the burst of flavors and the nutritional goodness in every bite!
